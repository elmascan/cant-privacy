CanT Privacy Policy

Can Train with Your Mate anywhere

1. Introduction

Welcome to CanT ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the CanT mobile application.

By using CanT, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Full name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Profile Information: City, bio, sports interests, and profile photo that you choose to provide.
  • Location Data: Approximate or precise location to show nearby events and players (only when you grant permission).
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, screens viewed, and features used (via Amplitude analytics).
  • Communications: Messages sent within the app between matched users.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and push notification token for sending notifications.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To match you with other players who share your sports interests
  • To show nearby sports events on the map
  • To enable direct messaging between matched users
  • To send push notifications about matches, messages, and events
  • To improve the app through analytics
  • To ensure the safety and security of our community

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore, Authentication, and Storage), which is compliant with industry-standard security practices.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, or deletion.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase – Authentication, database, storage, and push notifications
  • Google Maps – Map display and location services
  • Amplitude – Anonymous usage analytics to improve the app

Each of these services has its own privacy policy and data processing terms.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in the following situations:

  • With other users as part of the app's matching and community features (e.g., your name and profile photo)
  • With service providers listed above who help us operate the app
  • If required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users

7. Location Data

CanT requests access to your device's location to show nearby sports events and players. Location access is optional — you can use the app without granting location permission, though some features may be limited.

We do not share your precise location with other users.

8. Children's Privacy

CanT is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for location access at any time via device settings

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email below.

10. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the bottom of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
