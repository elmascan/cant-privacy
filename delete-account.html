How to Delete Your Account

You can request account deletion using either of the following methods:

Option 1 – Via the App

  1. Open the CanT app and sign in
  2. Tap your profile avatar in the top right corner
  3. Go to Settings
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Option 2 – Via Email

Send a deletion request to our support team:

Email Us to Delete Account

Please include the email address associated with your CanT account. We will process your request within 7 business days.

What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently deleted within 30 days:

  • Your profile information (name, bio, city, sports interests)
  • Your profile photo
  • Your event history (events you created or joined)
  • Your matches and direct messages
  • Your swipe history
  • Your community memberships and messages
  • Your push notification token
⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be lost.

Data We May Retain

After account deletion, we may retain the following data for legal or safety purposes:

  • Anonymized analytics data – retained indefinitely (cannot be linked back to you)
  • Transaction records – retained for up to 7 years if required by law
  • Abuse/safety reports – retained for up to 2 years to protect our community
